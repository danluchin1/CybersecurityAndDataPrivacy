# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Resource Booking System** (the "System") collects, uses, and protects your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 1. Data Controller
The data controller responsible for your personal information is:
* **Company Name:** CentriaCyber

## 2. Data We Collect
We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the booking service:
* **Account Information:** Username, email address, and password (hashed).
* **Eligibility Data:** Age (to verify you are over 15 years old).
* **Booking Data:** Resource ID, date, and time of reservation.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data based on the following:
* **Contractual Necessity:** To manage your account and fulfill your resource bookings.
* **Legal Obligation:** To verify age requirements for users (15+).
* **Consent:** Provided by you at the time of registration.

## 4. How We Use Your Data
* To allow you to register, log in, and book resources.
* To allow administrators to manage resources and troubleshoot system issues.
* **Public Visibility:** We do NOT display your identity to non-logged-in users. Publicly, only the resource status (e.g., "Booked") is visible.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or if an administrator removes your profile, your personal identifiers will be erased from our active database in accordance with the "Right to be Forgotten."

## 6. Your Rights under GDPR
Under GDPR, you have the following rights:
* **Right of Access:** You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
* **Right to Rectification:** You can update your profile information at any time.
* **Right to Erasure:** You can request that we delete your account and personal data.
* **Right to Withdraw Consent:** You may withdraw your consent for data processing by closing your account.
These rights are not yet technically implemented in the UI.

## 7. Data Security
We implement "Privacy by Design" principles, including:
* Secure password hashing (e.g., bcrypt/Argon2).
* Role-based access control (Reservers vs. Administrators).
* Protection against common web vulnerabilities (CSRF, XSS).

## 8. Cookies
We use essential session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

*For inquiries regarding your data, please contact the System Administrator via the contact details provided above.*
