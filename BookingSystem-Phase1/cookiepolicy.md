# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** March 14, 2026

This policy explains how our Booking System uses cookies to provide a secure and functional experience.

## 1. What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They allow the system to "remember" your session and security preferences.

## 2. How We Use Cookies
We use only **Strictly Necessary Cookies**. These are essential for the operation of the system and include:
* **Session Cookies:** Used to identify you while you are logged in so you don't have to re-authenticate on every page.
* **Security Cookies:** Used to prevent Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) attacks.
* **Load Balancing Cookies:** Used to ensure the application remains stable and responsive.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies
We do **not** use any third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel).

## 4. Managing Cookies
Most web browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. However, please note that if you disable essential cookies, you will be unable to log in or book resources, as the system will not be able to maintain your secure session.
